Privacy Policy for Radiant

Last updated: December 28, 2025

Privacy Summary: Radiant stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information on external servers.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Radiant ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the Radiant mobile application (the "App").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following information locally on your device using secure local storage (AsyncStorage):

  • Journal Entries: Your daily gratitude entries, affirmations, and reflections
  • Vision Board Images: Photos you upload or take within the app
  • Activity Data: Your check-in history, streak count, and progress insights
  • Settings: Your notification preferences and customization choices

2.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Any data on external servers

3. How We Use Your Information

All data collected by the App is used exclusively on your device to:

  • Display your journal entries and vision board
  • Track your daily check-in streaks and progress
  • Send you local notifications based on your preferences
  • Provide personalized affirmations and insights

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage, a secure local storage system provided by React Native. Your data never leaves your device.

4.2 Data Security

Your device's operating system (iOS or Android) provides encryption and security for locally stored data. We do not have access to your data.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

5.1 Expo Framework

The App is built using Expo, a framework for React Native apps. Expo does not collect personal data unless you explicitly enable analytics features (which we have not enabled).

5.2 Device Permissions

The App requests the following permissions:

  • Camera: To take photos for your vision board (only when you choose to use this feature)
  • Photo Library: To select existing photos for your vision board (only when you choose to use this feature)
  • Notifications: To send you daily reminders based on your preferences (only if you enable notifications)

All permissions are optional and only used for the specific features described.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose your data to any third parties. Since all data is stored locally on your device, we have no access to it.

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Access and Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • View, edit, or delete your journal entries at any time
  • Remove vision board images whenever you want
  • Change your notification settings in the app
  • Uninstall the app to permanently delete all your data

7.2 Data Deletion

To delete all your data, simply uninstall the App from your device. This will permanently remove all locally stored information. We cannot recover this data as we do not have server-side backups.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has used the App, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Posting a notice in the App (for significant changes)

Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

10. Future Features

If we add features that involve data transmission or external services (such as cloud backup, social sharing, or premium features), we will:

  • Update this Privacy Policy before implementing those features
  • Notify you of the changes
  • Obtain your explicit consent where required

11. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: support@radiantapp.com
  • GitHub: https://github.com/tinyseeker/radiant-app

12. Legal Compliance

12.1 GDPR Compliance (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to access your data (all data is already accessible to you in the app)
  • Right to deletion (uninstall the app)
  • Right to data portability (currently not available, but may be added in future updates)

12.2 CCPA Compliance (California Users)

If you are a California resident, under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • We do not sell your personal information
  • We do not share your personal information with third parties
  • You have the right to delete your data (uninstall the app)

13. Data Retention

Your data is retained on your device for as long as the App is installed. We do not retain any data on external servers. When you uninstall the App, all data is permanently deleted from your device.
